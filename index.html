Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2025

Introduction

Choradora ("we", "our", or "the app") is a family chore management app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and role (parent or kid) that you provide during signup.
  • Chore data: chores, rewards, points, streaks, and achievements you create within the app.
  • Photos: images you optionally attach as chore completion proof. These are stored in your private iCloud container.
  • Device identifiers: used solely for push notifications and syncing across your devices.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the app's core functionality: managing chores, rewards, and family accounts.
  • To sync your data across your devices via iCloud.
  • To send push notifications about chore reminders and updates.
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

  • All your data is stored in Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) under your personal Apple ID. We do not operate our own servers.
  • Sensitive credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain.
  • We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Children's Privacy

  • Kid accounts are created and managed by a parent within the app.
  • Kids do not provide an email address or any personal information beyond a display name chosen by their parent.
  • We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Subscriptions

  • Choradora offers an auto-renewable monthly subscription.
  • Payment is processed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not collect or store any payment information.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Third-Party Services

We use Apple's CloudKit, StoreKit, and Push Notification services. These are governed by Apple's own privacy policy. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app at any time.
  • Since all data is stored in your private iCloud container, deleting the app and your iCloud data removes everything.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page and within the app.
